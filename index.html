About us

We're selling mugs with memes, anime characters and animals printed on them. Our collection is still under development, so stay tuned!

Our first priorities are quality, customer satisfaction and reliability. We make our mugs with care as we believe mugs are a way to tell a person how grateful you are for something, how much you care about them, even if it's just a piece of porcelain in your cabinet.

You can search for a mug by opening the products tab. You can pre-order mugs that are not yet in stock. Our prices are competitive and reflect the quality of our products, that's why it might seem expensive. (See our "How do we define our prices?")

Our mugs are for any occasion, age or personality! You can order for a 8 year old, a teenager, a 85 years old or even for yourself!
